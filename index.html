Backstory

This is my li'l guy. He's a funny mosquito guy named Msqe (ponounced 'mos-kay') who drains souls, rather than blood. I made his concept over the summer, and did like 2 frames of animation before realizing i was bad, and stopping.
I used this assignment as an opportunity to actually finish animating him.
This is just his abdomen opening up and closing.
There would be some souls releasing from it, but the way i made the sprite sheet, they would have looked really ugly staying still when the abdomen was just open, which was something I didnt realize i wanted to do until after I had already made the spritesheet.
I still wanted to include the work that went into making them, so i threw them at the bottom. Just imagine them leaving the abdomen at some point while opening.

Controls (?)

Bubbling animation: B
Opening animation: O
Closing animation: C
Hopefully you enjoy! I sure would if i could get it to work the way I wanted it to! :/
